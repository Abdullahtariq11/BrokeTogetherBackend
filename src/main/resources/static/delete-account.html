Delete Your Account

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your BrokeTogether account and all associated data, please send an email to the address below with the subject line "Account Deletion Request":

Email: support@broketogether.app

Please include the email address associated with your BrokeTogether account so we can locate and delete your data.

What Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and password.
  • Household memberships: You will be removed from all households you belong to.
  • Expense records: Expenses you created will be disassociated from your account.
  • Balance data: All balance and settlement records tied to your account.

What May Be Retained

Certain data may be retained for a limited period after deletion:

  • Household expense history: Records of expenses and settlements involving you may remain visible to other household members for their bookkeeping purposes, but your personal information (name, email) will be removed or anonymized.
  • Encrypted backups: Your data may persist in encrypted backups for up to 90 days before being permanently purged.

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You will lose access to all households and expense history. Please settle any outstanding balances before requesting deletion.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests will be processed within 30 days of receiving your email. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.
